Seasons

There are 5 seasons of the original Steven Universe series, totaling 160 episodes. The franchise also consists of an additional season known as Steven Universe: Future and a feature-length film known as Steven Universe: The Movie.

Characters

The main protagonist is the titular Steven Universe, who is accompanied by Garnet, Amethyst and Pearl. The main antogonists of the series are the diamonds.

Music

There are two official Steven Universe soundtracks, volumes 1 and 2, released in 2017 and 2019 respectively. Collectively, the volumes have a total of 49 tracks, excluding any released with Steven Universe: Future and Steven Universe: The Movie.
